Why H_art The Band?

Hart the Band started off in 2012 when the three met at Kenya National Theatre during a Cancer Awareness Day. The trio realized that they shared the same passion in music, spoken word, poetry, fashion and arts generally.Their love for poetry and artistic feature makes them a favorite.
